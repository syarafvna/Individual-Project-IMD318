The first author is @Elvrosethrones.She is a doctor who writes in her free time. I first discovered her on Wattpad. I really love all her works, as I find her writing perfectly suits my preferences. For me, the best work she has ever written is The King. Sadly, she chose to archive that story, but I am still patiently waiting for her to publish it again.

The next one is @Leena_syiha. All her works are in the Islamic genre, which I truly love because it’s rare nowadays to find an author who writes romance while still following Islamic guidelines. She is also a Wattpad-based writer but has now published more than three physical books.

Then we have @NurIzzahHani. She is a teacher who found her passion in the field of writing. Most of her books are based on uniformed unit themes, focusing on action-thriller genres with a touch of romance, which makes her works truly special. She has published more than five books, and their reprints show just how much people enjoy her writing.

The next author is @nurhafsah_hamid, a talented writer known for her inspiring and faith-centered stories. One of her remarkable works, Will You Stay?, is a beautiful exploration of love, faith, and the challenges of self-discovery. I deeply admire how she incorporates Islamic values into her storytelling, making her books not only enjoyable but also spiritually uplifting.
"Will You Stay?" is a book that left a lasting impression on me, as it portrays meaningful relationships while addressing the struggles of staying true to oneself and one’s faith. It’s a must-read for anyone looking for a heartfelt and relatable story.
